三、作文

作文的第一段写作：①引出主题 ②描述主题 ③举例子论证

以上三个大方面相结合，每一个大方面里有几种不同的可供选择的角度，每个角度又有几种不同的句型，根本不会有模板套用的倾向，所以放心大胆地组合使用吧！

一、引出主题

1：…引起关注

(1) …has attracted/drawn/ captured a great deal of public attention. /attention from the general public.

(2) Due attention should be given to…

(3) Much attention has been paid to…

2：…成为了重要的社会问题

(1) …has arisen as one of the essential issues.

(2) … emerge into one’s vision.

(3) … has been brought to our attention.

3：…引起争议

(1) There has been much controversy over (whether +句子)

(2) Opinions vary when it comes to…= in terms of 谈及到，关于

(3) …has caused quite a stir among the general public.

(4) As regards…, there has been much controversy over…

二、描述主题

4：…很重要，…很必要(结合经典开头)

经典开头：It cannot be denied that…不可否认的是…

It is beyond doubt that…毫无疑问…

It is generally accepted that…人们普遍认为…

It goes without saying that…众所周知…

There is a growing recognition that.… 越来越多的人认识到…

(1) …Play a key / an(increasingly) essential / indispensable/ fundamental/ important role.

(2) …be of utmost/ vital/crucial/ critical/paramount importance.

(3) Nothing is more important than…

(4) …be of great necessity (to do…

补充：①…和…都扮淡着重要的角色

__and__both play__role (in…)

②…和…同等重要.

__and__play an equally__role (in…)

让句子变长：**(1)加时间**：in this era, in this age, in this time(s)

(2)加地点：in modern/contemporary society; at school or in the workplace

(3)加人物：for us students; for the young generations

练习：In this era when science and technology is developing rapidly, it cannot be denied that innovation plays an essential role, boosting economic growth and social progress.

推动：promote、boost、facilitate、stimulate

文科： liberal arts /humanities

1.重视…他也可放在最后一段)

(1) ① pay ② attention to_

(2) ② attach ② importance to_

(3) ② lay ② emphasis /stress on_

①主语可以是 One (should)、We students、we youngsters

② special、great、considerable

90后: the post-90’s generation

5.…对于…产生了影响

(1) exert a positive / negative / great impact on…

(2) have a profound / huge/considerable / great impact / influence on…

小学教育：primary education

中学教育：secondary education

高等教育： higher education

6.…使得…；…让…；…带来

(1) allow /enable /render sb to do…

(2) offer sb sth/ offer sth to sb

provide sb with sth /provide sth for sb

bring sb sth

三.举出例子

7.越来越…

(1) An increasing/ a growing number of…+ tend to

(2) people in growing numbers + be likely to

(3) More and more people + be inclined t

(4)A mounting number of + be bound to

8.时间/地点见证了…/在某时某地发生了…

(1) The past several years have/has witnessed (a sharp increase / decrease in the number of_ who/which_).

例：A mounting number of parents are likely to send their kids to art classes.

改写：The past several years has witnessed an exponential increase in the number of parents who are likely to send their kids to art classes.

补充：第一段言作方法

·漫画类

①The picture/cartoon displays a very special scene：描述图片

②simple as it is, what the picture conveys to us is thought-provoking：含义

→①句可替换成：In the picture we could see clearly that__.

②句可替换成：Conspicuously, this cartoon symbolically characterizes a status quo:__.

·名言类：

①It’s often said that: / Just as the saying goes, / There is an old saying going like this, 重述名言.

②Simple as the remark may sound, it intends to tell us that / inform / emphasize / indicate / highlight that 解释含义

→①②之间可以加上：（用来凑字数）

(1) Even though it is the wisdom of our ancestors, this remark can also be applied to many cases even today.

(2) Although this spark of wisdom has been buried in history for a number of years, it can also be applied to many situations even today.

(3) Even though it is the experience of our forefathers, this saying can be used in many causes today.

·图表类.

柱图：bar chart、线图：line chart、饼图：pie chart、表格：table

① The graph provides some interesting data regarding_(图表内容).

② A glance at the bar chart reveals_(可以直接加词，that加句子) . + specifically,__(具体)

③ This is a line graph which describes the general trend of_(名词).

④ The given table offers a glimpse of _(直接加单词).

比例/百分比：ratio / percentage / proportion

第二段写作构思小技巧

写作总体套路：

万能背景句+衔接句+点题+作用/解决

作用+举例子/背景句+解决/措施/设想

万能背景句⇔现象⇔解决 / 打算怎样做⇔ 联想到背景句体现的原因，最后再总体想一想逻辑对不对

一、开头句

1.解决方案类：

To achieve this goal, in my mind, there are at least two approaches.

2.现象解释类：

(1) Many fundamental factors are involved in understanding this phenomenon/their importance / this remark/ saying/principle. （灵活可变）

(2) There are mainly two factors contributing to this social phenomenon / the correctness of the principle / the importance of….（灵活可变）

(3) The reasons for this social phenomenon can be listed / are as follows.

(4) Two fundamental factors/ reasons could account for this….

(5) When it comes to the significance of this phenomenon, I feel it necessary to discuss the matter further.

(6) With regard to the implication of this phenomenon, my discussion is generally twofold.

二、万能过渡小短句

1.“两点”怎么表达？

(1) To begin with, Furthermore

(2) First and foremost, In addition

(3) In the first place, Besides

2.顺接/转接

因果衔接: As a result；As a consequence；consequently；therefore

转折关系: However；Nevertheless；Nonetheless；In spite of this

3.“总结”

总之：In conclusion；In a word；To sum up + 解决方式/展望未来

简而言之：In short；In brief

4.个人观点

To my know ledge；As for me；As far as I am concerned

万能背景句（主要用于第2段+联系自己想到的）

一、经济类

经济发展

\1. Owing to the steady and rapid economic development of our country, people’s living standard is simultaneously（同时） improving at a fast rate.

\2. In recent years, China is marked by rapid economic growth and continuous technological innovation.

补充：中国的改革开放政策：The reform and opening-up policy.

…是经济增长背后的驱动力：…is the driving force behind the economic growth.

\3. The past several years have witnessed a burgeoning（迅速发展的）economy in China, and the living standard of our society has been substantially（显著地）improved.

二、社会类

（一）社会压力

\1. In modern society where competition is intense, young people are under enormous strain from their family and work.

\2. With the quickening pace of urban life and ever- increasing pressure, people in growing numbers are suffering from either the physical or mental problems. +导致现象

补充：快节奏的生活方式：fast-paced lifestyle

在快速变化的世界里：In the fast-changing world

（二）就业困难

\1. As the job market is getting gloomy and competition is becoming fierce, it is increasingly difficult for college undergraduates to find a decent job. + However，…可以帮助找工作

\2. College graduates in China are having a hard time getting employed, more often than not, even on quite unappealing jobs.

补充：more A than B：是A不是B

（三）积极向上

\1. Traditional way of thinking has changed dramatically, and people are exhibiting greater open-mindedness and a burning desire to determine their own destiny.

\2. In contemporary society where opportunities abound, everybody embraces his or her own ambition, and most of us are willing to strive towards our realization.

三、技术类

（一）技术普及

\1. The internet has penetrated every aspect of daily life, greatly affecting our ways of communication.

\2. The computer is becoming an integral part in all fields of education and business.

（二）技术好处

\1. Technological innovation really brings us much convenience, exerting a huge impact on our traditional cultures.

\2. Technology helps to enhance the efficiency in people’s study and work, making our activities more economical and less time-consuming.

\3. The advent of the Internet opens up the possibilities of communicating and interacting via the computer.

\4. Modern technology considerably alters the way people communicate with each other.

（三）技术坏处

\1. The youngsters are more likely to be overly dependent on technology, even getting addicted to it.

\2. Technology could render city-dwellers slaves to high technology, alienating people from one another.

四、教育类

（一）教育好处

\1. Education can cultivate and elevate people’s mind, empowering us with confidence, courage and other virtues.

\2. Higher education plays a key role in aiding students’ growth and development, so that they can get the opportunity to increase their knowledge and learn to be independent.

（二）教育坏处

\1. In current education system, schools nowadays tend to pay special attention to the students’ academic scores rather than their morality.

\2. Most students usually concentrate merely on their school performance but neglect the other important aspects in life.

五、精神类

（一）精神品质

\1. People with the spirit of creativity can not only accumulate wealth, but also establish their career, fit into society, and even attain social status. （灵活多变，看情况写好处）

\2. Optimism can be regarded as a traditional Chinese virtue that has been handed down from generation to generation.

（二）人生哲理

\1. It is well-known that we exist in a dynamic world with various difficulties, we can do nothing but face them, Attitude is the key point to take the first step. （可变）

\2. The value of life lies not in the length of days, but in the use we make of them.

六、举例子

For example, ① honored for his/her success in ②, is always remembered for his/her spirit of ③, which shines through every chapter /page of his/her life.

①②③ The famous Chinese actor Jackson, music, innovation

①②③ The famous Chinese athlete Su Bingtian, 100-meter sprint, innovation

①②③ The Chinese academician Yuan Longping, developing the first hybrid rice in the world, innovation

七、万能理由/作用

1.提高工作效率：improve work efficiency

2.拓宽朋友圈：widen one’s circle of friends

3.放松心情，减验压力： relax one’s mind and release one’s pressure

4.创造和谐的工作/校园环境：create a harmonious working/ campus atmosphere

5.提供许多便利：offer us much convenience

6.拓宽知识面： enable one to expand his range of knowledge

7.恢复精力：enable us to restore our energy

8.增强人际交往/动的力：enhance interpersonal/interactive skills

9.提供机会找到一份体面的工作：offer us opportunities to seek a decent job
